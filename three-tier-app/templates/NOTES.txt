3-Tier Application Deployed Successfully!

Architecture:
  - Frontend  : Nginx         (NodePort 30080)
  - Backend   : http-echo     (ClusterIP :5678)
  - Database  : MySQL 8.0     (ClusterIP :3306)

Access the Frontend:
  minikube service {{ include "three-tier-app.fullname" . }}-frontend --url

Or manually:
  export NODE_PORT={{ .Values.frontend.service.nodePort }}
  export NODE_IP=$(minikube ip)
  echo "Frontend URL: http://$NODE_IP:$NODE_PORT"

Check all pods are running:
  kubectl get pods

Check all services:
  kubectl get svc
